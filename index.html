JOSS Contracting specializes in the protection of commercial and residential construction from the harsh environmental conditions of the State of Florida. Our expertise in waterproofing and application of sealants ensures that water and moisture will not penetrate your construction areas that need to stay dry. We are also experts in barrier technology application. We use the very best products from the best manufactures available on the market today. We are the leader in designing and installing high-grade waterproofing, air barriers, vehicular traffic coating systems, concrete joint sealants, sandwich slabs, planter insulation, amenity decks and vault waterproofing. The forty five inches of rain that falls in Florida each year is not something to take lightly. Do not trust just anyone to keep your structures dry.
